Nicecafe At Nelson Bay

Nice at Nelson Bay is a newly opened cafe in Nelson Bay. Offering Breakfast, Lunch, Beverages and Light Snacks you are sure to find something to take your fancy. We source our coffee from Jasper in Melbourne and use as much local produce as possible. Our juices are freshly squeezed and majority of our desserts are hand made. Poached eggs are our breakfast specialty and we have some of the best tasting salt and pepper squid in town. Come and have a taste!
